Baked Spaghetti

Comforting baked spaghetti with plenty of melted cheese, the perfect dish for potlucks, family gatherings, or a weeknight dinner.

Baked spaghetti is the easy, comforting, and crowd-pleasing dinner you're looking for

Baked Spaghetti Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Sauce
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Cheeses
  • Butter

How to make Baked Spaghetti

  1. Boil and drain the spaghetti
  2. Cook the beef and onion together, then drail off excess oil
  3. Add the sauce and salt
  4. Whisk the eggs, parmesan and butter
  5. Toss the spaghetti in the parmesan mixture
  6. Layer the ingredients in a prepared baking dish
  7. Cover and bake for 40 minutes
  8. Sprinkle with mozzarella
  9. Keep baking until the cheese is melted
